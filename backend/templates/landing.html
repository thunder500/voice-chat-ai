Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

A complete voice AI platform that runs entirely on your hardware. No data leaves your network.

Voice Chat

Talk naturally with an AI assistant. Real-time speech-to-text and text-to-speech with sub-second response times and multiple voice options.

Meeting Recording

Record Google Meet sessions with automatic speaker identification, real-time transcription, and AI-generated meeting summaries.

Multi-Language

Speak and transcribe in over 50 languages. Automatic language detection and seamless switching between languages in real-time.

Upload documents and build a private knowledge base. The AI references your files for accurate, context-aware answers using RAG.
